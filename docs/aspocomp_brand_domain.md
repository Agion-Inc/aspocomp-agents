# Aspocomp Brand and Domain Documentation

## Company Overview

**Aspocomp Group Plc** is a Finnish PCB (Printed Circuit Board) manufacturer with over 45 years of experience, serving global customers across 42+ countries. The company specializes in high-quality, reliable PCB production for various industries including automotive, semiconductor, telecom, industrial electronics, and defense/aerospace sectors.

## Domain-Specific Terminology

### Technical Terms
- **PCB**: Printed Circuit Board
- **HDI**: High-Density Interconnect
- **FR4**: Flame Retardant 4 (PCB substrate material)
- **Tg**: Glass Transition Temperature
- **QTA**: Quick Turn Around (fast delivery service)
- **IMS**: Insulated Metal Substrate
- **Via**: Electrical connection between PCB layers
- **Laminate**: PCB substrate material
- **Prepreg**: Pre-impregnated composite fiber

### Business Terms
- **RFQ**: Request for Quotation
- **QTA**: Quick Turn Around (fast delivery)
- **Design Support**: Engineering assistance services
- **Global Supply Services**: Worldwide distribution network

## Customer Journey

1. **Discovery**: Customer learns about Aspocomp capabilities
2. **Inquiry**: Customer submits RFQ (Request for Quotation)
3. **Consultation**: Design support and technical consultation

6. **Manufacturing**: PCB production in Oulu facility
7. **Quality Control**: Testing and certification
8. **Delivery**: Logistics and shipping to customer
9. **Support**: Ongoing customer service and support
